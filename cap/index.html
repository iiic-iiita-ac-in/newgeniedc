IIITA Info Communication and Incubation Centre (IIIC) has launched the Campus Ambassador Programme. An opportunity for students all over India to be a part of IIIC, IIIT Allahabad and act as a link between IIIT Allahabad and your own campus. As a Campus ambassador, you would be the representative of IIIC in your college campus amongst all the Campus Ambassadors from all over India in the networking events throughout the year. Campus Ambassador Programme is a chance for all of you to get involved in committed networking and a learning curve of Entrepreneurship in your student career.

Eligibility Criteria:

Any student from any college ranging from Freshers to the Final year can apply for the Campus Ambassador Programme.
Campus Ambassadors would be chosen after a brief procedure thereafter for shortlisted candidates.
In short, any student all over India who has an Entrepreneurial aptitude towards his life is a good enough candidate to be a Campus Ambassador!

RESPONSIBILITIES:

• Acting as a link between IIIT Allahabad and your own campus.
• Publicising Events and Competitions related to Entrepreneurship in your college as well as over Social-Media.
• Interacting with Alumni Entrepreneurs of your college and invite them to E-SUMMIT 2022 as one of the Guests.
• Networking with local Start Ups and Start up Co-Founders.
• Coordinating with Associate Members’ team of IIIC, IIIT Allahabad to conduct E-SUMMIT 2022.
• Publicity and formation of student contingent of your campus visiting E-SUMMIT 2022.

PERKS/ Reasons to join:

• Certificate of Appreciation signed by Professor-In-Charge IIIC, IIIT Allahabad after successful completion of tenure.
• Networking with our Alumni Entrepreneurs and local Start up Ecosystem.
• Improve your communication and management skills by being a part of the largest student network in the nation.
• Opportunity to attend all E-summit events for free.
• Goddies to top performers of the program.

So, avail a chance to stand apart from the crowd and make the change you want to see in your Institute
